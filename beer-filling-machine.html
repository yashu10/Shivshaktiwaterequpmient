Bottle Filling Machines

Beer Filling Machine

Oxygen-Free Bottling for Craft and Commercial Breweries

The greatest enemy of fresh beer is oxygen. The Shiv Shakti Beer Filling Machine is engineered with rigorous double pre-evacuation technology designed to flush bottles with CO2 multiple times before filing. This dramatically reduces dissolved oxygen (DO) pickup, drastically extending the shelf life and preserving the authentic flavor profile of your brew. Combining a long-tube filling mechanism to minimize agitation, and ultra-hygienic automated capping/crowning, it is the ultimate, affordable scaling solution for microbreweries and large-scale beer manufacturers alike.

  • ✓ Double pre-evacuation vacuum flushing
  • ✓ Long-tube low-agitation liquid dispensing
  • ✓ Supports Crown Corking and ROPP/Screw Caps
  • ✓ Extremely low Total Packaged Oxygen (TPO)

Features & Benefits

  • ✓
    Double Pre-Evacuation Evacuates air, flushes inert CO2, evacuates again. Ensures <50ppb oxygen pickup.
  • ✓
    Foam Stimulation (Fobbing) Controlled micro-jet of water post-fill causes foam to rise, pushing out remaining headspace air.
  • ✓
    Versatile Crowning High-speed magnetic or mechanical crowning heads built for heavy glass beer bottles.
  • ✓
    Sanitary Layout Sloped bases and zero dead-leg piping absolutely prevents microbial buildup in the brewhouse.

Technical Specifications

Production Capacity 1000 to 12000 Bottles Per Hour
Bottle Type Standard Glass Beer Containers (330ml, 650ml)
Capping Type Crown Corking / ROPP Aluminum / Twist-off
Vacuum Pump Liquid ring extremely high-suction vacuum
Oxygen Pickup (TPO) Consistently under 0.05 mg/L
Sanitation Standard 100% Steam-Sterilizable (SIP) capable
