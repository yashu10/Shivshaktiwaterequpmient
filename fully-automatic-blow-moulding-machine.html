Pet Blow molding Machine

Fully Automatic Blow Moulding Machine

Uninterrupted High-Speed Bottle Manufacturing

Eliminate the massive logistical costs of transporting empty bottles by manufacturing them exactly when you need them. The Fully Automatic PET Stretch Blow Moulding Machine takes compact, cheap PET preforms and magically stretches and blows them into finalized bottles at intense speeds. Featuring an automatic preform hopper, infrared heating ovens with independent zone control, and heavy-tonnage pneumatic clamping, this machine connects directly via air conveyors to your filling line. It is the pinnacle of vertical integration for modern packaging plants.

  • ✓ Fully automated preform feeding to bottle ejection
  • ✓ Advanced Infrared (IR) rotary heating ovens
  • ✓ Servo-controlled stretching rods for uniform thickness
  • ✓ High-pressure air recuperation system (Saves Power)

Features & Benefits

  • ✓
    Total Vertical Integration Never depend on third-party bottle suppliers again. Drastically lower your cost-per-liter.
  • ✓
    Perfect Wall Thickness Independent heating lamps and advanced stretching profiles ensure minimum weight bottles with maximum crush resistance.
  • ✓
    Rapid Mold Swap Slide-out mold mounting designs allow you to switch bottle shapes (e.g., from 500ml to 1000ml) in under an hour.
  • ✓
    Air Recovery Captures the intense exhaust pressure after a blow cycle and re-routes it to drive the mechanical pneumatic cylinders.
