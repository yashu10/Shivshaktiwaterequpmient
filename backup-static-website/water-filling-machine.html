Bottle Filling Machines

Water Filling Machine

High-Speed Monoblock Water Bottling Solutions

SHIV SHAKTI WATER EQUIPMENT PVT. LTD. manufactures heavy-duty, fully automatic Mineral Water Filling Machines designed for modern bottling plants. Our reliable 3-in-1 monoblock system integrates washing, filling, and capping into a single, compact framed unit. Built completely from SS 304/316 contact parts, it ensures 100% hygienic water packaging suitable for pack sizes ranging from 200ml to 2000ml. Engineered for minimal downtime and maximal precision, our filling lines help you achieve incredible production throughput while strictly adhering to international purity standards.

  • ✓ Output capacity from 20 BPM up to 300 BPM
  • ✓ Suitable for 200ml to 2000ml PET/Glass bottles
  • ✓ Integrated Neck Handling Technology
  • ✓ Advanced PLC Touchscreen Control

Features & Benefits

  • ✓
    No Bottle, No Fill System Smart optical sensors ensure the valves only open when a bottle is securely positioned, saving water wastage.
  • ✓
    Magnetic Capping Head Provides constant torque to prevent cap damage and ensures absolute leak-proof sealing.
  • ✓
    PLC Interface Control Fully programmed with a touchscreen HMI for easy speed adjustment, volume tracking, and fault diagnosis.
  • ✓
    SS 304/316 Construction Food grade stainless steel is utilized on all contact parts supporting GMP standard layouts.

Technical Specifications

Production Capacity 20 to 300 Bottles Per Minute (BPM)
Bottle Size Supported 200 ml to 2000 ml
Machine Construction Contact Parts: SS 316. Outer Body: SS 304
Power Requirement 3 Phase, 440 V, 50Hz (5 kW - 15 kW)
Automation Level Fully Automatic Multi-Head Machine
Control System Delta / Mitsubishi PLC with 7" Touch Screen
